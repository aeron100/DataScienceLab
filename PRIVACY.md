# Privacy Policy

**App:** DataScienceLab  
**Bundle ID:** com.datasciencelab.mac  
**Last updated:** April 8, 2026

---

## Summary

DataScienceLab does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data processing happens entirely on your device.

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

DataScienceLab does not collect:

- Personal information (name, email, location, etc.)
- Usage analytics or crash reports sent to us
- The contents of any files you load into the app
- Any information about your datasets, models, or analysis sessions

---

## Local Data Processing

All data science operations — file loading, preprocessing, machine learning model training, EDA, and export — are performed locally on your Mac using Apple's native frameworks (TabularData, CreateML, Accelerate). No data is transmitted to any server operated by DataScienceLab.

---

## AI Assistant

DataScienceLab includes an optional AI chat assistant with two provider options:

### Ollama (Local)
When using Ollama, all inference runs entirely on your machine. No data is sent externally. This is the default recommended option for maximum privacy.

### OpenRouter (Cloud)
If you choose to use OpenRouter, your messages and dataset context are transmitted to OpenRouter's servers for processing. This is subject to [OpenRouter's Privacy Policy](https://openrouter.ai/privacy). Your OpenRouter API key is stored securely in the macOS Keychain and is never transmitted to DataScienceLab.

You are not required to use either AI provider. The AI Chat tab is entirely optional.

---

## File Access

DataScienceLab requests access to files only when you explicitly open or import them using the file picker or drag-and-drop. The app operates within the macOS App Sandbox and does not access files outside of those you select.

---

## Third-Party SDKs

DataScienceLab does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

---

## Children's Privacy

DataScienceLab does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no features designed to collect personal data from any user.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted to this page with a revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
